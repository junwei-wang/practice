The derivate of a line function $y=mx+b$ is $D_{x}y=m$. That is, the rate of
change of $y$ with respect to x is conatant.
\\
函数$y=mx+b$的导数是$D_{x}y=m$。也就是说，$y$对于$x$的变化率是一个常量。
